A Start-Up Love Story of Hiral & Abhishek (Co-Founders).

Our story began in 2010 when we met in Haridwar (UP, India) for a brief family Pilgrimage. After the togetherness of 10 days, we separated for full three years, and Destiny was kind enough to make us meet again in 2013 in an ARTS DEGREE COLLEGE, and this time it was for some big-good reasons & for the rest of this current lifespan. Since then, we are together figuring out each other’s strengths and weaknesses, and also we are trying hard to figure out LIFE.

We have learnt many things until now by doing different Jobs & Businesses. We tried our effort separately, but 2020 was the time to Shift The Gears.

Hlādinī Media took birth by gathering both’s Passion to Work in Design Industry (For Past 5 Years). We always believed to work hard for our dream by helping others to achieve theirs. Creative Thinking, Designing & Distribution was the fuel of our Vehicle called Life.

(Hlādinī means Pleasure Potency in Sanskrit) We tend to please our customers with accurate design work and out of the box digital marketing ideas through this venture.
